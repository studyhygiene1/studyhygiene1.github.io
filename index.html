About Us

We’re educators at heart, with degrees in Electrical Engineering (M.Sc.), Computer Engineering (B.Sc.), and Law (J.D., licensed attorney). We love what we do because it gives us the incredible opportunity to make a lasting difference in our students’ lives. At Study Hygiene, our teaching philosophy is holistic — blending content mastery, test-taking strategies, and learning psychology. We understand that consistent success comes from cultivating healthy and "hygenic" study habits. Having studied and taught in the American, British, and Asian systems of education, we have a deep understanding of effective learning strategies. Our students range from middle schoolers to seasoned professionals because learning is a lifelong pursuit.

For test prep, using diagnostics, we identify and fill gaps in essential content knowledge before diving into timed practice drills and high-impact test-taking strategies. We train students to strategically leverage their use of Desmos and other tools. We’ve helped many students score 1500+ on the SAT, 32+ on the ACT, and excel on many other standardized tests.

We guide students through every stage of their college essay — from brainstorming to final polish. As gifted orators, we also coach public speaking and interviewing. In short, we can help you overcome anxiety, find your voice, and communicate with confidence — in any medium.

We know what it’s like to face challenges. We once struggled with math ourselves, and we know how transformative the right guidance can be. In over 25 years of tutoring, we’ve helped thousands of students. They’ve been admitted to top universities and built thriving careers as engineers, doctors, lawyers, scientists, and executives.
